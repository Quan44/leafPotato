🔍 How Our Model Works

Our potato leaf disease classifier uses a deep learning model (EfficientNet B0) trained on thousands of images of potato plant leaves. The model analyzes patterns, colors, and textures in the leaf images to identify signs of various diseases.

The process works as follows:

  1. Image Upload: You upload an image of a potato leaf
  2. Preprocessing: Our system preprocesses the image (resizing, normalization)
  3. Analysis: The AI model analyzes the image features
  4. Diagnosis: The system provides a diagnosis with confidence levels
